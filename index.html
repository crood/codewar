About me:


My name is Christine Pomplun, and I am a passionate 3D/2D Artist, Designer, programming generalist, and amateur athlete.


My deep passion for digital art and visualization has lead me to study and work in this field.

I have studied in Munich (Germany) and New York (USA).  Where i have obtained the bachelor degrees in Graphic Design, Multimedia Design, and 3D Game Development.
